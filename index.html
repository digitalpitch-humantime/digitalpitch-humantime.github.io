Digital Pitch, Human Time is an experimental project where mathematical algorithms generate a series of musical pitches. The rhythm and harmony is completely human-determined, resulting in a composition involving both mathematical principles and human participation.

Each week, 2 to 3 approximately minute-long new compositions will be put up on this website. You can listen to them by clicking on the “Compositions” button on the menu bar.
